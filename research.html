Non-Dilutive Funding

We target immense pools of federal R&D capital—specifically SBIRs, STTRs, and grants. This is non-dilutive funding, meaning the government funds your research, but you keep 100% of your company's equity and IP rights.

The federal acquisition landscape (especially DARPA and DoD OTAs) is a minefield of red tape, compliance regulations, and complex proposal writing. We step in as your dedicated Project Manager and Prime Contractor partner.
