The Core Training Modules

Phase 1 through 4 lays the unshakeable foundation of your enterprise.

Phase 01

Executive Architecture

We eliminate the red tape and confusion of federal registration. Before you can win a contract, you must exist securely within the government's ecosystem. We walk you through establishing your business entity correctly to avoid severe tax liabilities and compliance audits down the line.

Key Outcomes

  • LLC vs. S-Corp structures for federal work.
  • Step-by-step SAM.gov error-free registration.
  • Securing your UEI and CAGE Codes.
  • WOSB, SDVOSB, and 8(a) certification roadmaps.
Phase 02

Digital Ecosystem & Identity

The government buys from entities they trust. If a Contracting Officer lands on your website and it looks unprofessional, your proposal goes in the trash. We teach you how to build a digital footprint that screams capability, stability, and mission readiness.

Key Outcomes

  • Identifying and defining your exact federal niche.
  • Architecting a federal-ready website layout.
  • Designing a 1-page Capability Statement that converts.
  • Gathering endorsements without prior federal past performance.
Phase 03

The Acquisition Hunt

Money is constantly flowing through SAM.gov, DLA, and agency-specific portals. We teach you how to read the complex language of the government, filter out the noise, and identify solicitations that you have a high mathematical probability of winning.

Key Outcomes

  • Advanced filtering on SAM.gov and DIBBS.
  • Decoding RFP, RFQ, and Sources Sought notices.
  • Responding effectively to Requests for Information (RFIs).
  • Navigating DARPA and Other Transaction Authorities (OTAs).
Phase 04

Proposal Engineering

This is where contracts are won and lost. We pull back the curtain and give you the exact technical writing strategies, compliance matrices, and pricing models that PINK PILLED™ uses to secure prime contracts.

Key Outcomes

  • Building foolproof Section L & M Compliance Matrices.
  • Technical writing tailored for federal evaluators.
  • Cost-volume strategies to ensure profitability.
  • Internal red-teaming and proposal review processes.
Phase 05

Career Prep & Strategic Positioning

Winning a contract is only part of the equation; you must also know how to operate in the room. This module prepares you to present yourself as a formidable Prime Contractor. We put you through the wringer with live mock cold calls, pretend capability meetings, and high-pressure mock contract scenarios so you are fully battle-tested before speaking to actual federal personnel.

Key Outcomes

  • Federal-focused resume and LinkedIn optimization.
  • Live mock cold calls to Contracting Officers and Primes.
  • Pretend meetings and Capability Briefing simulations.
  • Navigating high-stress mock contract negotiation scenarios.

Theory is useless without execution. Upon completing the core modules and establishing your entity, you enter the Shadowing Phase. You don't just get a certificate—you get access to our real-world operations.

  • Live Proposal Breakdowns with our Executives

  • Sub-Contractor Pipeline Evaluation for PINK PILLED™ Primes
